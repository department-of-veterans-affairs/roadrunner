Getting Started!

Roadrunner was designed to help the Digital Service team at the VA and Digital Service partners including VA employees and contractors build projects that will be migrated into Vets.gov and Appeals. The purpose of this open source style guide is to standarize our products, scale out team and accelerate our deliveries. When building a new app, team members can use the Roadrunner Github and help continue to build and upgrade the repository.

Our Goals

  • Unifying and simplifying VA’s digital touch points, dramatically improving customer experience
  • Meeting customer need on the first try, on any device
  • Creating a mobile-first platform
  • Retooling existing applications and designing new end-to-end experiences, in partnership with business owners across VA
  • Providing transparency about our methods and processes
